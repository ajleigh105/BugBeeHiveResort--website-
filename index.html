About Us

The Bug-Bee Hive Resort is a third generation, all-season resort located just 90 minutes west of Minneapolis on Bee-Utiful Lake Koronis, in the heart of central Minnesota's lake country. Families enjoy our upscale cabins, swimming beach area, water trampoline, "Busy Bee" kid's play-town area, indoor swimming pool and our summer time weekly activities program. Our Lodge & Retreat Center is ideal for weddings, reunions, business meetings, banquets, scrapbooking and gatherings of all kinds.
